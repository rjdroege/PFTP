Power from the People

Power from the People is a site created to help you track your elected representatives and help you hold them accountable for their work in representing you.

To use this site, please start by creating an account. This will allow you to save your representatives and will remove the need for you to search for them on each visit. Once you have created an account, you will search for your representatives using your home address. This is a work in progress. Please check back in the near future for updates.
